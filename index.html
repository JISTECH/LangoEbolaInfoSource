About Ebola Virus Disease(EVD)

Ebola virus disease (also known as Ebola hemorrhagic fever) is a rare and deadly disease that periodically causes outbreaks in several African countries. It is spread by contact with blood or body fluids of a person infected with Ebola virus. It is also spread by contact with contaminated objects or infected animals Ebola Virus Disease has got the following signs and symptoms, which when not reacted to can lead death with the first few days of discovering the signs and symptoms.

Ebola Virus Disease what it is?

Ebola is a rare but deadly virus that causes fever, body aches, and diarrhea, and sometimes bleeding inside and outside the body.
In Ebola outbreaks, too many people still arrive at health centres with advanced disease or even die at home, infecting others in the process. This is what we need to avoid.

HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF FROM DEADLY EBOLA VIRUS DISEASE(EVD)

The best way to avoid Ebola is to stay away from areas where the virus is common.
If you are in an outbreak area: Avoid infected people, their body fluids, and avoid handshake body contact with the infected person.

WEAR FACE MASK

Put on a fabric face mask to protect you from the fluids from an infected person, when in public. Discard the face mask after use in a right place if its a disposable one
